Mark Lee

Mark Lee, born on August 2, 1999, in Canada, is a Canadian-Korean singer, rapper, and dancer, best known as a member of NCT and its sub-units NCT 127 and NCT Dream. From a young age, Mark showed a deep passion for music and performance, excelling in singing, rapping, and dancing. He joined SM Entertainment as a trainee and trained for several years, honing his skills and stage presence. Mark is recognized for his charismatic performances, sharp rap skills, and dedication to his craft. Beyond music, he is admired for his hardworking attitude, creativity, and ability to inspire fans worldwide. Throughout his career, Mark has contributed to numerous hit songs and captivating performances, making him one of the standout talents in K-pop today.
